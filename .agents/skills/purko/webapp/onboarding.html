Agents — your AI staffcommunity

Specialists, not a chatbot: each has a role, a model, and real tools via MCP — registries, document stores, your intake system. You define them once; the whole team can use them.

2

Workflows — work, orchestratedcommunity

Multi-step pipelines with parallel branches, retries, and webhook or scheduled triggers. The contract arrives, the webhook fires, four agents get to work — no copy-paste choreography.

3

Human gates — you stay in chargecommunity

Nothing irreversible happens without a signature. Runs pause at the steps you mark, and your decision is recorded with your name and a timestamp.

4

The ledger — a defensible audit trailpro · full history

Every tool call, every output, every approval — logged. When someone asks "how did you reach this conclusion," you answer with the chain, not a shrug. Pro keeps the full searchable history.

5

Memory — a team that rememberspro

Agents recall what they learned in past runs — your brand voice, your clause preferences, last quarter's findings — instead of starting from zero every morning.

6

Hosted studio & SSO — we run the machinespro

A managed workspace on this cluster: your team signs in with Google or GitHub, roles decide who may approve and who may only watch, and upgrades are our problem.
